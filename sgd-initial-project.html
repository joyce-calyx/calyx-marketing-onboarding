Overview

The Baseline Sales Enablement Kit is the Senior Graphic Designer's Initial Project — a complete set of foundational sales assets that give the Calyx sales team what they need to tell the brand story, communicate product value, and move conversations forward at every stage of the funnel.

This kit doesn't exist yet in a complete, current, brand-accurate form. Building it requires the designer to synthesize everything learned during the first 60 days of onboarding — product knowledge, brand guidelines, production workflows, and sales priorities — into five polished deliverables. The project is scoped and kicked off within the first 90 days. Full completion follows in Q3.

Success is not just about design quality. It's about how the SGD manages a multi-asset project with cross-functional contributors, incorporates sales feedback into the creative, and ships work that the team actually uses.

Project type
Cross-functional design production — five distinct assets with shared brand and messaging requirements.
Sponsoring manager
Alex Joyce, Sr. Marketing Manager. Provides strategic direction, messaging input, and final sign-off on all assets.
Primary owner
Senior Graphic Designer. Responsible for project management, design production, and coordinating all contributor input.
Target format
All assets delivered in final production-ready files, with editable source files stored in the Calyx Marketing Drive design asset library.
Objectives

The Baseline Sales Enablement Kit has four objectives — each maps to a real gap in how the sales team currently operates.

1
Give the sales team a complete, on-brand asset library The sales team should never have to use outdated, off-brand, or piecemeal materials in a customer conversation. This kit establishes the baseline they'll use going forward.
2
Create a durable template system for future updates Every asset should be built in a way that allows future updates without starting from scratch. Templates, defined styles, and source files stay in the design asset library.
3
Demonstrate how the SGD manages a multi-asset production project This project is also an evaluation of how the designer scopes, coordinates, handles feedback, and ships. The process matters as much as the output.
4
Build a foundation for the Calyx rebrand The brand system and visual decisions made in this kit will inform the broader rebrand being scoped. Getting the baseline right matters beyond just these five assets.

Deliverables

Five assets make up the Baseline Sales Enablement Kit. Each requires distinct inputs from different contributors — gathering those inputs is part of the designer's project management responsibility.

1
Master Sales Deck
Presentation

The primary brand and product story deck used by the sales team in customer-facing conversations. Covers company overview, product portfolio (Flexible Packaging, Rigid Packaging, Calyx Cure, Labels), key differentiators, and a call to action. Built to be modular so reps can pull relevant slides for different conversations.

Messaging: Alex Joyce Product content: Peter Andersson Sales feedback: Kyle Bissell Format: Google Slides + PDF export
2
Product One-Sheeter
Print + Digital

A single-page visual reference covering the full Calyx product portfolio — one asset that gives a prospect a complete picture of what Calyx offers. Designed to work both as a printed leave-behind and a digital PDF send. Concise, visually strong, and brand-accurate.

Messaging: Alex Joyce Product specs: Peter Andersson Print specs: Amie Hadley Format: Print-ready PDF + digital version
3
History of Calyx
Print + Digital

A brand story asset that traces Calyx's founding, growth, and mission. Used in sales conversations to build credibility and emotional connection. Should feel premium and editorial — not a corporate timeline. The narrative is co-developed with Alex Joyce and Simon Knobel; the design and format belong to the SGD.

Narrative: Alex Joyce + Simon Knobel Photography: existing brand library Format: Printed booklet + PDF
4
Price Sheets
Print + Digital

Formatted, brand-accurate price sheets for the relevant product lines. Design must be clean, easy to read, and updatable — pricing changes, and the format needs to accommodate those updates without a full redesign. Specific pricing content is provided by the sales team; the SGD owns layout, formatting, and brand consistency.

Pricing content: Kyle Bissell + sales team Product categories: Peter Andersson Format: Editable template + PDF export
5
Nurture Email Campaigns
HubSpot

A set of designed, branded email templates for nurture sequences built and deployed in HubSpot. The SGD owns the visual design and template structure; Alex Joyce owns messaging strategy and copy. Templates must be responsive, on-brand, and functional within HubSpot's email builder. Coordinate with Xander Ward for HubSpot setup, list segmentation, and deployment.

Copy + strategy: Alex Joyce HubSpot setup: Xander Ward Segmentation: Xander Ward + sales team Format: HubSpot email templates

Milestones

The kit is built in three phases. Phase 1 — scope lock and contributor alignment — is the only hard Day 90 commitment. Full production follows in Q3.

Day 61–65
Kick off & scope initiation
SGD proposes kit scope and asset list to Alex Joyce. Confirm all five deliverables, identify contributors per asset, and schedule input-gathering sessions with each contributor.
Day 66–74
Contributor sessions & content gathering
Structured sessions with Peter Andersson (product content), Kyle Bissell (sales feedback and pricing), Simon Knobel (History of Calyx narrative), and Alex Joyce (messaging and copy direction). HubSpot setup session with Xander Ward. Notes and briefs documented after each session.
Day 75
Scope document approved — Phase 1 complete
One-page scope document signed off by Alex Joyce. Confirms all five assets, contributors, content inputs received, design direction agreed, and Phase 2 production timeline set. This is the Day 90 checkpoint commitment.
Post Day 90 · Phase 2
Asset production & contributor review
All five assets designed and in review. Each contributor reviews for accuracy in their area. Feedback incorporated. Alex Joyce approval received on each asset before it's considered final.
Post Day 90 · Phase 3
Kit delivered & deployed
Final assets delivered to the sales team. Source files and templates added to the design asset library. Nurture email campaigns live in HubSpot. Kit announced to the team. Assets added to the SGD onboarding checklist for future hires.
